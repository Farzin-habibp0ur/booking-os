# Platform Launch Configuration

> Last updated: 2026-03-12
> Strategy: Phased rollout — concentrate on 3 primary channels before expanding.

---

## Phase A — PRIMARY PLATFORMS (Months 1-2)

**Status: ACTIVE**
**Launch date: 2026-03-17 (Week 1)**

### Instagram

- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Launch date:** 2026-03-17
- **Initial cadence:** 5 posts/week (3 Reels, 1 carousel, 1 static)
- **Stories:** 3-5/day
- **Ramp-up schedule:**
  - Weeks 1-2: 3 posts/week (calibration — test formats, hashtags, posting times)
  - Weeks 3-4: 4 posts/week (add carousel format)
  - Weeks 5+: 5 posts/week (full cadence)
- **Primary content types:** Reels (demo clips, tips), carousels (feature breakdowns, before/after), Stories (polls, BTS, social proof)
- **KPIs:** Follower growth rate, engagement rate, Reels reach, link clicks

### TikTok

- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Launch date:** 2026-03-17
- **Initial cadence:** 5 videos/week
- **Ramp-up schedule:**
  - Weeks 1-2: 3 videos/week (test hooks, formats, sounds)
  - Weeks 3-4: 4 videos/week (double down on top-performing format)
  - Weeks 5+: 5 videos/week (full cadence)
- **Primary content types:** Short-form video (15-60s), tutorials, myth-busting, trend-jacking, founder story
- **KPIs:** Views, completion rate, follower growth, shares

### LinkedIn

- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Launch date:** 2026-03-17
- **Initial cadence:** 4 posts/week
- **Ramp-up schedule:**
  - Weeks 1-2: 2 posts/week (establish voice and audience signal)
  - Weeks 3-4: 3 posts/week (add document/carousel posts)
  - Weeks 5+: 4 posts/week (full cadence)
- **Primary content types:** Text posts (founder insights, lessons), document carousels, polls, comment engagement
- **KPIs:** Impressions, engagement rate, profile views, connection requests, demo signups

## Phase B — SECONDARY PLATFORMS (Months 3-4)

**Status: LOCKED**
**Projected launch: 2026-05-12 (earliest)**

### Unlock Criteria (ALL must be met):

- [ ] Instagram followers >= 500
- [ ] Instagram engagement rate >= 2%
- [ ] TikTok followers >= 500
- [ ] TikTok engagement rate >= 2%
- [ ] LinkedIn followers >= 500
- [ ] LinkedIn engagement rate >= 2%
- [ ] Consistent posting for 8+ weeks (no more than 2 missed posts in any week)

### YouTube

- **Status:** LOCKED
- **Post-unlock cadence:** 2 long-form videos/week + 3 Shorts/week
- **Ramp-up schedule (post-unlock):**
  - Weeks 1-2: 1 long-form + 2 Shorts/week (50% cadence)
  - Weeks 3-4: 2 long-form + 3 Shorts/week (full cadence)
- **Primary content types:** Tutorials (3-10 min), product walkthroughs, industry commentary, Shorts (repurposed TikToks/Reels)
- **KPIs:** Watch time, subscriber growth, CTR, Shorts views

### Pinterest

- **Status:** LOCKED
- **Post-unlock cadence:** 10 pins/week
- **Ramp-up schedule (post-unlock):**
  - Weeks 1-2: 5 pins/week (50% cadence)
  - Weeks 3-4: 10 pins/week (full cadence)
- **Primary content types:** Infographic pins, blog post pins, idea pins (repurposed carousels), product feature pins
- **KPIs:** Impressions, saves, outbound clicks, follower growth
