## Budget Rules

1. **Never spend on paid amplification before Month 3** — need organic baseline first
2. **Only boost content with proven organic engagement** — top 10% performers by engagement rate
3. **Track CAC by channel** — kill any channel with CAC > $50 after 30 days of data
4. **Reallocate budget monthly** based on Performance Analyst data and customer validation report
5. **Emergency fund:** Keep 1 month of tool costs ($187-329) in reserve at all times
6. **Influencer rule:** Only pay for micro-influencers (1K-50K followers) in our verticals. No macro-influencers until MRR > $5K
7. **Paid amplification cap:** Never exceed 50% of total monthly budget on paid — organic must remain primary
8. **Tool evaluation:** Review tool ROI quarterly. Cancel any tool not used in the previous 30 days
