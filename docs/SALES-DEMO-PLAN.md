## Act 1: The Command Center (5 min)

### 1.1 Dashboard — "Your clinic at a glance"

**What to show:**
- KPI strip at the top: today's bookings, revenue, no-show rate, consult-to-treatment conversion
- Action cards from AI agents — show one "Urgent Today" card (e.g., waitlist match or retention nudge)
- Click an action card to show the pre-generated follow-up message

**What to say:**
> "The moment you log in, you see exactly what needs attention today. These action cards are generated by AI agents running in the background — they detect at-risk customers, open waitlist slots, and scheduling gaps, then tell you exactly what to do about it."

### 1.2 Global Search — "Find anything instantly"

**What to show:**
- Press `Cmd+K` to open the command palette
- Type a customer name (e.g., "Emma") — show results across customers, bookings, conversations
- Type "new booking" to show quick actions

**What to say:**
> "Everything is searchable from one place. Customers, bookings, conversations, even actions like creating a new booking."

---

## Act 2: Day-to-Day Operations (8 min)

### 2.1 Calendar — "Your clinic's heartbeat"

**What to show:**
- Switch between Day / Week / Month views
- Click a booking to show the detail popover (status, customer info, service, notes)
- Show color-coded statuses: confirmed (green), pending (purple), in-progress (amber)
- Show staff filter — toggle different providers on/off
- Drag a booking to reschedule (conflict detection fires if overlapping)

**What to say:**
> "Your entire team's schedule in one view. Drag to reschedule, click to see details. Conflicts are caught automatically — no double-bookings."

### 2.2 Bookings — "Track every appointment"

**What to show:**
- Filter by status chips (Confirmed, Pending, No-Show)
- Show the date range presets (Today, This Week, This Month)
- Click a booking row to open detail modal — show status change, add notes
- Show CSV export button

**What to say:**
> "Filter, sort, export. Every booking has a full audit trail — who booked it, when, any status changes, deposits collected."

### 2.3 Customers — "Know your clients"

**What to show:**
- Search for a customer (e.g., "Emma Wilson")
- Click into customer detail page
- Show the **Intake Card** — allergies, contraindications, concern areas, medical flags
- Show booking history and total spend
- Show conversation history across channels

**What to say:**
> "This is built for aesthetic clinics. Medical intake, allergies, contraindications — all visible to your staff before the client walks in. You can see every conversation across WhatsApp, SMS, email, all in one place."

---

## Act 3: Messaging & AI (7 min)

### 3.1 Inbox — "Every conversation, every channel"

**What to show:**
- Show the conversation list with channel badges (WhatsApp, SMS, Email icons)
- Click into a WhatsApp conversation — show message thread
- Show the **channel pills** at the bottom — click to switch reply channel
- Show the **channel filter bar** at the top (ALL, WhatsApp, SMS, Email, etc. with unread counts)
- Type a reply but don't send — show the **draft auto-save** (blue dot appears on pill)

**What to say:**
> "Six channels — WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, SMS, Email, and live Web Chat — all in one inbox. Your staff never needs to switch between apps. Drafts auto-save, so nothing gets lost."

### 3.2 AI Suggestions — "Your AI assistant"

**What to show:**
- Find a conversation with an AI draft (lavender/purple bubble with dashed border)
- Show the AI draft with Approve & Send / Edit / Reject / Regenerate buttons
- Click Edit to show it loads into the composer with "Editing AI draft" banner
- Show the AI confidence indicator (green/amber/red dot)

**What to say:**
> "The AI reads every inbound message, detects intent — is this a booking request? A cancellation? A general question? — and drafts a reply for your staff. They can approve with one click, edit, or regenerate. You control the autonomy level: suggestions only, auto-with-review, or fully autonomous."

### 3.3 AI Settings — Quick mention

**What to show:**
- Navigate to Settings > AI
- Show the autonomy toggles: per-intent and per-channel overrides
- Show the usage stats (today's processed, auto-replied, failed)

**What to say:**
> "You decide how autonomous the AI is. Start conservative — suggestions only. As trust builds, turn on auto-reply for specific intents or channels."

---

## Act 4: Growth & Automation (5 min)

### 4.1 Campaigns — "Reach your clients at scale"

**What to show:**
- Show the campaigns list (draft, sent, scheduled)
- Click into a sent campaign to show the delivery funnel (Sent > Delivered > Read > Booked)
- Show the "New Campaign" button — briefly show the 4-step wizard (Channel > Audience > Message > Schedule)
- Show merge variables: `{{customerName}}`, `{{serviceName}}`

**What to say:**
> "Send targeted campaigns via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. Segment by tags, booking history, or last visit date. Track delivery all the way to conversion — did they actually book after receiving the message?"

### 4.2 Automations — "Set it and forget it"

**What to show:**
- Show the automation rules list (e.g., "24h Reminder", "Aftercare Follow-up", "Consult Follow-up")
- Click into one rule to show the trigger + action configuration
- Show the Logs tab — execution history with outcomes

**What to say:**
> "Booking confirmed? Automatic reminder 24 hours before. Treatment completed? Aftercare instructions sent within the hour. Consultation done? Follow-up in 3 days to convert to treatment. All automatic, all customizable."

### 4.3 Waitlist — "Never lose a slot"

**What to show:**
- Navigate to Waitlist page
- Show entries with status badges (Active, Offered, Booked)
- Explain the AI agent auto-match: "When a booking is cancelled, the waitlist agent finds the best match and generates a slot offer"

**What to say:**
> "Cancellations don't mean lost revenue. The AI matches cancelled slots to your waitlist automatically and drafts the offer message. Your staff just approves."

**Key points to reinforce:**

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We already use [competitor]" | "Does it give you AI-powered reply drafts across 6 channels? Consult-to-treatment conversion tracking? Waitlist auto-matching? Most platforms handle booking OR messaging — we do both, integrated." |
| "Our team won't adopt it" | "The setup wizard takes 20 minutes. Staff see only what's relevant to their role — providers see their calendar, agents see the inbox. And the AI handles the repetitive work." |
| "It's too expensive" | "The ROI dashboard tracks recovered revenue from reduced no-shows alone. Most clinics recover the subscription cost in the first month." |
| "What about data migration?" | "We support CSV import for customers. Booking history can be imported too. We'll help you get set up." |
| "Is it secure?" | "HTTPS everywhere, encrypted cookies, tenant isolation, brute force protection, WCAG accessibility compliance. Your data is completely separated from other businesses." |
